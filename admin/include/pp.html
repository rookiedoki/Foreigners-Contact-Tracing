FOREIGNERS CONTACT TRACING SYSTEM

Privacy Policy

Foreigners Contact Tracing is a contact tracing system that asks for several questions to foreigners who are recently arrived in Nabua.

An email notification will be sent to all who may have come in contact with an identified Covid-19 positive patient so that they can observe precautions and start self-isolation if symptoms occur to protect their families and loved ones.

Foreigners Contact Tracing System is a project of Team Brogrammers on the subject System Integration and Architecture 1 in guidance of their insructor Ms. Xaralyn Catimbang

Foreigners Contact Tracing aims for full transparency about the personal data that it processes when you use this application. Pursuant to the Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act), this Privacy Statement explains, among other things, what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use the information.

The Privacy Statement applies to all end users of Foreigners Contact Tracing System specifically and exclusively the Foreigners

1. WHAT DATA WE COLLECT

DATA RELATING TO END USERS

Some of the information we process may be considered personal data under applicable privacy legislation. This personal information includes:

  • - User name/ ID – is a unique identifier to be used by individual to login to Foreigners Contact Tracing System.
  • - Password – to secure the individual account in Foreigners Contact Tracing System. Only the registered individual can see and modify their password.
  • - Last Name, First Name – used for proper verification when Foreigners QR codes are being scanned and to be used by authorities for successful contact tracing.
  • - Contact number – so authorities will be able to contact the individual when doing contact tracing.
  • - Country/Town City of Residence – authorities may group based on location when doing contact tracing.
  • - E-mail Address - to be used by the user in retrieving their password.
  • - ID picture that is locally saved in the mobile phone – used for visual confirmation when Foreigners Contact Tracing QR Code are being scanned.
  • - Device’ Camera – to be used by the system for users’ to capture a picture for his/her profile and for reading Foreigners Contact Tracing QR Codes. Captured picture will be saved to device’ local storage and will be used for visual confirmation of identity of the user when his/her QR Code is being scanned by the authorities. This system does not record video when using the device’ camera.
  • - Device’ Storage, Files and Images – will only be accessed as an option by the user to upload his/her profile picture. This system has no capabilities to use or access videos and/or any other file types stored in the device. It will access the device local storage, upon user approval, to store the uploaded profile picture and transaction data. Transaction data will be used by the authorities when doing contact tracing while profile picture will not be uploaded to the server.

We only process your data for purposes that are objectively justified by Foreigners Contact Tracing application and only as stated in this Privacy Statement. We process this data with respect to your right to privacy, including the need to protect personal integrity and private life and to ensure that your personal data is adequately protected against the risk of loss, misuse and unintended alteration.

Unless you are offered the option to provide prior consent to our use of your data, we process your data with legal basis in the DATA Privacy Act (DPA) RA 10173 - Section 12. Criteria for Lawful Processing of Personal Information. We collect data for the contact tracing of individuals and all the persons whom he got in contact with, given that they are registered under the Foreigners Contact Tracing App

3. HOW WE COLLECT DATA

Generally, we collect data:

  • When you provide them explicitly to us, during your registration. Username and password are used for system log-in validation and password can be changed anytime by the users.

When we collect your data at end user level, we saved your critical information locally such as the your QR Code, your picture, your personal information including your health status. This string equivalent of your collected data are encrypted when transferred to our Foreigners Contact Tracing server.

We ask the following permissions specifically to get your ID picture that will be saved locally in your mobile phone:

Access to Photo File. Foreigners Contact Tracing also requests access to individual’s photo to allow selection of image or picture during registration that will form part of his identification together with the QR Code.

Captured image via phone’s camera upon the individual’s permission or copied image from phone’s photo file, also upon the individual’s approval, will be saved locally to individual’s phone together with his generated QR Code and personal profile. Foreigners Contact Tracing retains the captured ID picture or image locally in the individual’s phone for his identification. ID picture or uploaded image is not part of the file data in the server that Foreigners kept for contact tracing.

6. RETENTION AND DELETION

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for processing it in accordance with the purposes described in this statement, or as otherwise necessary to comply with applicable laws. We retain the history of your scanning transactions for only (30) thirty days. When your data is no longer necessary or relevant for our purposes, or required by applicable laws, we take steps to have it deleted.

7. ACCESS, CORRECTION AND DELETION OF PERSONAL DATA

You have the right to access the personal data you have registered about you. For profile information, if any of the information you have registered is incorrect, we encourage you to make changes to your profile (Last Name, First Name, Email, Location/ City/ Country, Cellphone number). You may also withdraw any consents you have given at any time. Feel free to contact us for assistance at telephone number (02) 8403-98-11 or email us at brogrammersFCTS@gmail.com

Personal Information of Individual will be retained in the Masterfile until the individual deletes its own personal record via mobile app. Personal Information of the individual together with his scanning history shall be deleted locally in his mobile app immediately upon deletion of his record. However, his personal record and corresponding scanning transactions will be retained in the server for thirty (30) days which will be used by the organization or agency for contact tracing. His record will be automatically deleted from the server after thirty (30) days from the date of its deletion from the mobile app.

Individual scanning transactions will be retained for thirty (30) days and will be automatically deleted by the system after thirty days.

8. YOUR RIGHT TO FILE A COMPLAINT

If you believe that we process your data in violation of your rights, you have the right to complain. You may always contact us directly at telephone number (02) 8403-98-11 before sending a complaint to the National Privacy Commission (NPC) office, so that we can try to solve or clarify the issue.
